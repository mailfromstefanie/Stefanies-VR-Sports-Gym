# Daily Support System

Use Europe/Amsterdam time.

At the first substantive project session of the day, keep the check brief:
1. What is today's one concrete project goal?
2. Is energy green, orange or red?
3. What is the smallest step that still moves the release forward?

## Energy choices
- Green: learning block plus planned project work.
- Orange: learning block or one small project step.
- Red: recovery, basic care and prepare only the next step.

## Work rhythm
- Give only one implementation step at a time.
- Test immediately after meaningful changes.
- On workdays, aim to finish technical work around 21:30.
- Do not start a risky extra task late in the evening.
- A difficult day does not mean the week has failed.

## Session close
Record:
- what was built or designed;
- what was learned and decided;
- why the direction is sound;
- the exact smallest next step.
